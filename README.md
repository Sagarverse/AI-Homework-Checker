AI Homework Evaluator
Instantly grade assignments, provide personalized feedback, and empower students to learn faster. This AI-powered tool is designed to streamline the evaluation process for educators and enhance the learning experience for students.

📖 Overview
The traditional homework grading process can be time-consuming for teachers and often involves a long wait for students to receive feedback. The AI Homework Evaluator is designed to solve this problem by providing immediate, consistent, and detailed feedback on a wide range of assignments.

By leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning models, this tool can understand the context of answers, evaluate their correctness, and offer constructive suggestions for improvement. It acts as a personal tutor, available 24/7 to help students identify their mistakes and understand complex concepts.

✨ Key Features
🚀 Instantaneous Grading: Get real-time results for assignments, eliminating manual grading time.

🧠 Intelligent Feedback: Provides more than just a score. The AI offers explanations, suggests improvements, and links to relevant learning resources.

📚 Multi-Subject Support: Capable of evaluating a variety of subjects, including math, science, literature, and history.

🚫 Plagiarism Detection: An integrated checker to ensure academic integrity and originality in student submissions.

📊 Performance Analytics: A dashboard for educators to track class-wide and individual student performance, identifying common areas of difficulty.

🔧 Customizable Rubrics: Teachers can define their own grading criteria and rubrics to align the AI's evaluation with their curriculum.

🔒 Secure & Private: Student data is handled with the utmost care, ensuring privacy and security.

🛠️ How It Works
This project is built on a modern tech stack designed for scalability and performance.

Submission: Students upload their homework through a clean and simple user interface.

Processing: The assignment is sent to our core AI engine, which uses a combination of Large Language Models (LLMs) and custom-trained models.

Evaluation: The AI analyzes the submission against the correct answers and the teacher's rubric. It assesses factual accuracy, logical reasoning, and writing quality.

Feedback Generation: The system generates a detailed report including a score, specific feedback on each question, and personalized learning recommendations.

Results: The student receives the report instantly, and the results are populated on the educator's dashboard.
